Frequently Asked Questions

1. How far in advance should I book a portable toilet rental in USA?

It's always best to book your portable toilet rental as soon as your event or project dates are confirmed. For larger events or during peak seasons (spring/summer), we recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability of your preferred units, especially luxury portable restrooms for rent or bathroom trailer rentals. For urgent needs, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

2. What is the typical portable toilet rental cost in USA?

The portable toilet rental cost varies depending on several factors: the type of unit (standard, deluxe, ADA, luxury porta potty), the rental duration (daily, weekly, monthly), the number of units required, the frequency of servicing, and the delivery location. For specific porta potty rental prices, we recommend contacting us for a personalized quote.

3. How many portable restrooms do I need for my event or site?

The ideal number of units depends on the number of guests/workers, the duration of the event/project, and whether food or alcohol will be served. As a general guideline for events, consider one standard portable toilet for every 50-100 guests for a 4-hour event. For construction sites, OSHA recommends one toilet for every 10 workers for a normal work week, with frequent servicing. We can help you calculate the precise number when you contact us.

4. Are your portable restrooms cleaned regularly?

Absolutely. Hygiene is our top priority. All our portable restrooms undergo thorough cleaning and sanitization before delivery. For rentals, routine servicing is included, typically weekly, which involves pumping the waste tank, cleaning and sanitizing the interior surfaces, and restocking supplies like toilet paper and hand sanitizer. For high-traffic events or long-term rentals, more frequent servicing can be arranged.

5. Do you offer luxury portable restrooms for weddings?

Yes! We specialize in providing elegant portable toilets for weddings and other upscale events. Our luxury portable bathrooms and restroom trailer rental options offer amenities like flushable toilets, vanity sinks with running water, climate control, and attractive interiors, ensuring your guests enjoy a comfortable and sophisticated experience.

Chattahoochee Hills, GA Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Chattahoochee Hills (formerly Chattahoochee Hill Country) is a city in southern Fulton County, Georgia, United States. As of the 2010 census, it had a population of 2,378 living in an area of just over 32,000 acres (130 km2). The population in 2019 was estimated to 3,318 in an area of approximately 37,473 acres (151.65 km2) acres after subsequent annexations. It is the incorporated part of a region called "Chattahoochee Hill Country", an area encompassing approximately 60,000 acres (240 km2) southwest of Atlanta, bordered on the northwest side by the Chattahoochee River. Unlike the rest of metro Atlanta, it is still relatively undeveloped, and most of its rural character remains unchanged. The majority of the wider area comprises the west-southwest part of southern Fulton, and smaller adjacent parts of southern Douglas, eastern Carroll, and northern Coweta counties.
